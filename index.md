## Outside of Work

I live in the San Francisco Bay Area and recently became a parent, which has been a joyful and perspective-changing experience.

I stay closely connected to the civic technology community through volunteer work. I contribute to organizations such as U.S. Digital Response and the Aspen Tech Policy Hub, where I help support projects that improve how governments deliver services. I also teach financial literacy workshops for recent college graduates and serve on the board of a public service fellowship program that helps early-career professionals enter government work.

In addition to volunteer work, I occasionally provide pro bono support to local governments, academic institutions, and nonprofit organizations on data product development and service design. I also take on a limited number of consulting and development projects with private-sector clients.

Outside of technology and analytics, I enjoy hands-on hobbies that get me away from screens. I like refurbishing furniture, collecting stationery, and chopping firewood.
