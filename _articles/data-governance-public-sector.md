## Why governance matters

Public-sector analytics depends on reliable data.

Without governance, organizations face:

- inconsistent definitions
- duplicated datasets
- unreliable reports

## Core governance components

Strong data governance typically includes:

- clear data ownership
- documented data definitions
- standardized reporting processes
- data quality monitoring

## Building a governance culture

Governance is not only technical — it is organizational.

Successful programs combine:

- leadership support
- training for analysts
- shared standards for reporting
