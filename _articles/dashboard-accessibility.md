## Why accessibility matters

Public-sector dashboards must be usable by everyone, including users with visual or cognitive impairments.

Accessible dashboards improve:

- comprehension
- usability
- trust in government data
- decision-making speed

## Key accessibility principles

Some important design practices include:

- high contrast color palettes
- clear typography
- colorblind-safe visual encoding
- avoiding unnecessary visual clutter

## Practical checklist

Before publishing a dashboard, ask:

- Can someone interpret this chart without relying on color?
- Are labels readable at small screen sizes?
- Does the layout guide the user to the most important insight?

Accessibility is not just compliance — it is good design.
