## Start with the decision

Dashboards should answer a **specific question**.

Before building visuals, clarify:

- What decision should this dashboard support?
- Who is the audience?
- What actions should the viewer take?

## Design principles

Strong dashboards usually follow these principles:

- prioritize the most important metric
- reduce unnecessary visuals
- group related information
- use consistent colors and labels

## Avoid common mistakes

Common dashboard problems include:

- too many visuals
- inconsistent scales
- decorative charts without clear purpose

Good dashboard design focuses on **clarity over complexity**.
